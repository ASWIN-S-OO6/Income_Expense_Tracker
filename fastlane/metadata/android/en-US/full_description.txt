Income Expense Tracker is an open-source application designed to help users manage monthly income and expenses efficiently.

Features:
- Separate profiles for individual users and company accounts
- Separate monthly books for organized financial tracking
- Add, edit, and delete income and expense entries
- View monthly summaries and financial records
- Data stored locally on the device (offline support)
- Import and export functionality for backup and data transfer

Privacy:
This app stores all data locally on the device, does not use analytics or tracking services, does not include advertisements, and works completely offline.
